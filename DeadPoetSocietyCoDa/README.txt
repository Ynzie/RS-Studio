NEXT STEPS (DLC Builder)
========================
1. Open DeadPoetSocietyCoDa.rs2dlc in Rocksmith DLC Builder.
2. Audio: click the wand to calculate volumes, and use
   'Create preview audio' for the preview file.
3. The chart expects 10s of silence before bar 1.
   If your audio starts immediately, pad it in DLC Builder
   or re-export with a different lead-in.
4. Tones are preset starting points - tweak them in the Tones
   tab or import better ones from your tone collection.
5. Validate, generate DD, and Build.
